Organisation

Divisions

Five specialised teams — engineering, observation, operations, science, and administration — delivering ZINGSA’s national mandate as one agency.

01Space Engineering 02Geospatial & Earth Observation 03Space Operations & Launch 04Space Science 05Governance & Administration

Space Engineering

Provides engineering advice and assistance to ZINGSA programmes, innovations for space from concept to applications, and educates scientists and engineers to inspire space research.

Engineering disciplines

  • Electrical Engineering — power systems, circuit design, and electronic components
  • Aeronautical Engineering — UAV design, development, and operations
  • Mechanical Engineering — structural design and thermal management
  • System Engineering — integration of complex space systems
  • Electronics, Mechatronics & Radio Electronics
  • Aerospace Engineering — spacecraft design and orbital mechanics

Services & technologies

  • Satellite Development: CubeSats and small satellites using 3D printing
  • UAV Technology: Drones for precision agriculture, disaster assessment, and mapping
  • Ground Systems: Satellite communication centres and ground stations
  • Testing & Validation: Vibration, environmental testing, and system validation
  • Prototyping: Rapid prototyping with advanced manufacturing

Geospatial & Earth Observation

Specialised research and development on geospatial applications and Earth observation, implementing the national Earth Observation strategy and strengthening capacity in Zimbabwe.

Core services

  • Satellite Imagery Acquisition: Processing and disseminating space imagery
  • Geospatial Intelligence: Support for national planning
  • Remote Sensing: Environmental monitoring and resource management
  • GIS Services: Spatial data analysis and platforms
  • Mapping Services: Wetlands, agro-ecological zones, and land use

Application areas

Weather & ClimatePrecision AgricultureMiningDisaster ManagementWater ResourcesUrban Planning
  • Weather & Climate: Near-real-time prediction and sensor calibration
  • Agriculture: Yield estimation, crop health, soil moisture, agro-ecological mapping
  • Mining: Mineral exploration and activity mapping
  • Disaster Management: Flood, drought, veld fire detection and response
  • Environmental: Wetland mapping and land degradation monitoring
Applications hub NSDI

Space Operations & Launch Services

Specialised research on space exploration missions, satellite command and control, and facilitating access to space for ZINGSA missions.

Core functions

  • Earth observation mission preparation and implementation
  • Satellite command, control, and telemetry
  • Ground station operations and maintenance
  • Mission planning and launch access facilitation
  • System integration across complex operating processes

Infrastructure

  • Mazowe Satellite Center: State-of-the-art communications for satellite operations
  • Multiple ground stations for tracking, telemetry, and command
  • Secure communication networks and mission control centres
  • Data processing and distribution systems

Space Science

Champions research to increase understanding of space environments through scientific research and educational programmes that advance space science knowledge.

Research areas

  • Space Physics: Plasma physics and magnetospheric studies
  • Astrophysics: Celestial objects and cosmic phenomena
  • Space Weather: Monitoring and forecasting events affecting technology
  • Planetary Science: Planets, moons, and planetary systems

Ionospheric research

  • Ionospheric maps for Zimbabwe using GNSS techniques
  • Space weather forecasting and scintillation studies
  • Integration of Zimbabwean GNSS data into ionospheric models
  • Assessment of ionospheric variabilities and impacts
Space Weather page CORS network

Governance & Administration

Ensures efficient delivery of ZINGSA’s mandate through finance and administrative services so the organisation can focus on its core business.

Administration & finance

  • Strategic planning, budgets, and financial projects
  • Policy development and financial procedures
  • Resource management, reporting, and compliance

Human resources

  • Talent acquisition across space science and engineering
  • Capacity development and performance management
  • Knowledge management and workforce planning

Impact & collaboration

ZINGSA supports infrastructure development through advanced geospatial technologies, precision equipment, and partnerships across government, industry, and international organisations.

Applications

  • Construction surveying for roads, bridges, and urban projects
  • Mining operations mapping and environmental compliance
  • Land surveys, cadastral mapping, and geodetic surveys
