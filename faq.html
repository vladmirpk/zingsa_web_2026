Frequently asked questions

Short answers about ZINGSA’s mandate, services, satellites, and how to reach us.

01Questions 02Contact 03Book a service
What is ZINGSA?

The Zimbabwe National Geospatial and Space Agency is a wholly government-owned entity established under the Research Act [Chapter 10:22]. Officially launched in 2021, ZINGSA promotes the peaceful use of space and applies geospatial technologies for national development. Read about us.

What satellites has Zimbabwe launched?

ZIMSAT-1, Zimbabwe’s first satellite, entered service in November 2022 for drought monitoring, mining mapping, crop health, and weather support. ZIMSAT-2 expands national Earth observation capability. Programs.

How do I book drone or crop-spraying services?

Use the services booking pathway on our website or contact the operations team during office hours (Mon–Fri, 8am–4pm). Book a service or email publicrelations@zingsa.ac.zw.

How do I access the CORS network?

ZINGSA operates Continuously Operating Reference Stations for accurate GNSS positioning. See the CORS portal for network information and how to enquire about access. CORS portal.

What is NSDI?

The National Spatial Data Infrastructure standardises and integrates spatial data across sectors so government, industry, and researchers can plan with shared geospatial intelligence. NSDI overview.

Where can I see Earth observation applications?

ZINGSA applies space and geospatial data to agriculture, wetlands, disaster management, mining, and national planning. Applications hub.

What is space weather monitoring?

Space weather describes conditions that can affect GNSS, satellites, and communications. ZINGSA researches ionospheric conditions and scintillation over Zimbabwe. Space Weather.

Are there job openings?

Current vacancies are listed on the Careers page. When no roles are open, you can still send expressions of interest to recruitment. Careers · recruitment@zingsa.ac.zw.

How can the media or partners contact ZINGSA?

Call +263 8677009885 or email publicrelations@zingsa.ac.zw. Full contact details are on the Contact page.

Where can I download maps and documents?

Maps, program flyers, and the Client Service Charter (English, Shona, and Ndebele) are available from the Resources library. Open a charter to preview or download. Resources.

What is the Client Service Charter?

The Client Service Charter outlines ZINGSA’s service standards and the commitments you can expect from the Agency. It is published in English, Shona, and Ndebele. Preview or download.
