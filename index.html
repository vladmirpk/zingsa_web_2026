Our mandate

Built for Zimbabwe’s socio-economic future

ZINGSA is a wholly government-owned entity established under the Research Act [Chapter 10:22] and officially launched by His Excellency President Emmerson Mnangagwa in 2021.

We promote the peaceful use of space and apply geospatial and space technologies to support national development, industrialization, and informed decision-making across the country.
