13 July 2026

STEM school visits spotlight drones and satellites

ZINGSA educators and engineers hosted hands-on sessions for secondary learners, demonstrating UAV flight principles and how Earth observation supports farming and disaster awareness.

Outreach

28 April 2026

ZINGSA showcases capability at ZITF 2026

Visitors at the Zimbabwe International Trade Fair engaged with satellite models, CORS positioning demos, and precision-agriculture services offered by the Agency.

Events

10 October 2025

World Space Week brings space science to the public

Annual Space Week programming featured talks, demonstrations, and student engagement on peaceful uses of space and careers in geospatial science.

Events

15 August 2024

Presidential Innovation Award recognises ZINGSA partnership

ZINGSA, in collaboration with ZETDC, received the Presidential Innovation Award for outstanding contributions to technological advancement in Zimbabwe.

Milestones

7 November 2022

ZIMSAT-1 takes Zimbabwe into orbit

Zimbabwe’s first satellite entered service for drought monitoring, mining mapping, crop health assessment, and weather forecasting support — a landmark for national space capability.

Milestones

22 March 2021

Wetlands Master Plan strengthens national environmental mapping

ZINGSA’s satellite-led Zimbabwe Wetlands Master Plan 2021 delivered a comprehensive national view of wetland extent to support conservation and planning.

Milestones

10 July 2021

ZINGSA officially launched by the President

His Excellency President Emmerson Mnangagwa officially launched the Zimbabwe National Geospatial and Space Agency, marking a historic milestone in the nation’s space journey.

Milestones

18 June 2025

Public exhibitions highlight geospatial applications

Community exhibitions introduced visitors to agro-ecological mapping, disaster-assessment drones, and how space data informs everyday national decisions.
