Services

Precision services for Zimbabwe

Drone agriculture, GNSS survey support, and Earth observation mapping — delivered by over 15 certified pilots with 5,000+ flight hours since 2018.

Agriculture

Drone crop spraying

Farming season is here. Precision drone technology helps Zimbabwean farmers — commercial and small-scale — apply treatments accurately, protect yields, and cut waste, from flight planning through to spraying.

Precision application

GPS-guided spraying reduces waste and ensures optimal crop coverage.

Time efficient

Cover large fields quickly — up to 50 hectares per day.

Cost effective

Reduce operational costs while improving crop health and yields.

Expert service

Certified operators with extensive precision agriculture experience — trained through ZINGSA’s GIS and drone operations courses.

Human capital development

Drone training that powers the field.

ZINGSA’s GIS courses include certified drone pilot training, flight planning, and precision agriculture operations — the same skills behind every service we deliver.

Explore GIS & drone courses →

How it works

Smart aerial spraying for smarter farming

Precision, efficiency, and safety — from flight planning to spraying, empowering productivity in every field.

PrecisionTargeted, GPS-guided spraying for optimal results across every part of the field.
EfficiencySave time, reduce waste, and increase yield compared with manual or vehicle-based spraying.
SafetyMinimise human exposure to chemicals by keeping operators out of the spray zone.
01

Flight planning

The mission is planned — flight path and coverage set against the field boundary before the drone leaves the ground.

02

Pesticide filling

The drone is prepared and filled with the right solution and dosage for the crop and treatment.

03

Spraying

The drone executes precise, GPS-guided spraying for maximum, even coverage across the field.

04

ZINGSA fleet

Missions are powered by the ZINGSA drone fleet, scaled to the size of the field and the season’s demand.

05

Monitor & optimize

Performance is monitored and data analysed to optimise coverage and inputs on future missions.

Who benefits

Built for Zimbabwe, scaled to every farm

For Zimbabwe

Better crop coverage and healthier yields support national food security, reduce chemical drift into water and soil, and protect farmers and surrounding communities.

For commercial farms

Cover up to 50 hectares a day, cut labour and input costs, and use monitoring data to plan and optimise the next season’s spraying.

For small-scale farmers

Access precision spraying without owning or maintaining a drone — book a certified ZINGSA operator for a single field or a full season.
